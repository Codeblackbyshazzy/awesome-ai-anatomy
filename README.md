## 🎯 What Makes This Different

- **Architecture diagrams** — Mermaid + hand-drawn style, not just text descriptions
- **"Why" analysis** — Design decisions explained, not just "what they used"
- **Trade-off discussions** — Every choice has a cost. We discuss both sides.
- **Limitations identified** — We find potential issues, not just praise
- **Comparison with alternatives** — How does this compare to other approaches?
